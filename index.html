What's inside

Built for Battle

Every system — physics, AI, rendering, audio, progression — written from scratch in Java. No Unity, no LibGDX, no shortcuts. Just raw Java2D and 5,500+ lines of code doing the heavy lifting.

🚀

Jetpack Combat

Limited fuel, recharges on ground. Aerial fights change everything.

🤖

Smart AI Bots

Track targets, lead shots, dodge, retreat at low HP. 3 difficulty levels.

⭐

Progression System

30 levels, XP, coins, unlockable skins, camos, trails and death effects.

👥

Team Mode

Co-op vs bots, human vs human, or mixed teams. Personal and team modes.

🎮

Local 2-Player

Shared keyboard. P1 uses arrow keys, P2 uses WASD. Works with team mode.

💾

Named Profiles

Create multiple profiles. Your stats, XP and unlocks persist across sessions.
